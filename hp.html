Developed by a group of students from STI College Las
Pinas, IT211, a meal planner desktop application that will
aim to monitor the food expenses of the students by
allowing them to plan their meals in advance.



In this Meal Planner, you were able to set and
pick your own food plan, pick a day with just one
click. Planning your food right away can save your time.



With this application you were able to set your
own food plan, pick your choices of foods and
drinks and set it for the next day.
